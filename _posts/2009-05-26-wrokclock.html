I had to see it for believing it.

Our Ministry of the Interior put a work clock at our social insurance web services. In other words our social insurance institute can not service us through the web from 7:00am till 3:00pm. Let me check, ooh yes. We live in 2009.

The above images says: "For your better service, our web services are available everyday from 3:00pm to 7:00am . Thank you for your understanding."
